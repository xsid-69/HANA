# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**Customer (Client):** Young professionals (22-35) in Indian cities looking for platonic companionship experiences. They want to discover interesting people to share activities with — coffee, walks, cultural outings, professional chats. They browse on mobile-first, emotionally driven, looking for connection and quality experiences.

**Companion (Service Provider):** Skilled, verified professionals offering their time and expertise as companions. They manage a small personal business — scheduling, earnings, bookings, reviews. They need efficiency, clarity, and a sense of professional empowerment.

## Product Purpose

HaNA is a two-sided companionship marketplace. Customers discover and book companions for platonic social experiences. Companions manage their availability, earnings, and client relationships. Success means customers feel excited to explore, and companions feel in control of a growing business.

The two sides share brand identity but deliver completely different products:
- Customer: social lifestyle discovery app (Uber Rider, Airbnb Guest)
- Companion: premium business management platform (Uber Driver, Airbnb Host)

## Brand Personality

**Warm, Premium, Playful + Bold, Intimate, Modern**

Voice: confident but never cold. Direct but emotionally intelligent. Premium without being exclusive. The brand speaks like a trusted friend who happens to have impeccable taste.

Emotional goals:
- Customer side: excitement, curiosity, warmth, trust
- Companion side: empowerment, pride, clarity, growth

## Anti-references

- Generic dating apps (Tinder/Bumble swipe UI, gamified matching, cheap card stacks)
- Corporate SaaS dashboards (Salesforce gray grids, enterprise data tables, clinical productivity)
- Escort/adult platforms (anything transactional, commodifying, or seedy)
- Generic creator dashboards (Gumroad/Patreon template feel)

## Design Principles

1. **Two products, one soul.** Customer and companion experiences feel distinct but unmistakably related — like siblings, not strangers.
2. **People over pixels.** Photography, personality, and human connection drive every layout decision. Data serves people, not the other way around.
3. **Earned complexity.** Start simple, reveal depth. The companion dashboard shows what matters now; analytics unfold on demand.
4. **Emotional precision.** Every interaction has a feeling. Discovery feels like anticipation. Booking feels like commitment. Earning feels like progress.
5. **Confident restraint.** Premium means knowing what to leave out. Bold color, generous whitespace, purposeful motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA compliance (4.5:1 contrast for body text, 3:1 for large text)
- Full keyboard navigation
- Screen reader support with semantic HTML and ARIA where needed
- Reduced motion alternatives via prefers-reduced-motion
- Color-blind safe: never use color alone to convey meaning
